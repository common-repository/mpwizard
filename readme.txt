Create Mercado Pago payment links directly on your WordPress website.

== Description ==

Create payment links without having to log in to your Mercado Pago account. Put your products and services on sale quickly and safely.

With Mercado Pago your customers can pay by credit card, debit card, electronic transfer and means of payment in cash.

You can create payment links in test mode and then put them up for sale in production mode without having to create them again.

Check your payment link history at any time.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `mpwizard` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** screen (**Plugins > Installed Plugins**).

You will find **MPWizard** menu in your WordPress admin screen.

For basic usage, have a look at the [plugin's website](https://laudanumsoft.com/mpwizard-wordpress-plugin-documentation/).
